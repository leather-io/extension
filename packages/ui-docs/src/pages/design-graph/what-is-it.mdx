# The design graph

## What is it?

> Much of this is based off of the work of Jackson Black, and his thinking related to the
> [Design Graph](https://jxnblk.com/blog/design-graph/).

Essentially, the Design Graph is a way of thinking about design and UI in a constraint based way. You can think about
design as being built out of constraints, such as _Design Tokens_: scales of sizes, colors, and other variables.
